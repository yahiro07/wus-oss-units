how the sounds are made

everything is synthesised live from the web audio api. no samples on disk.

  • kick: sine oscillator starting around 120hz, pitched down to 50hz over 80ms with a snappy amplitude envelope.
  • snare: white noise through a bandpass at 1.8khz, mixed with a short triangle body around 200hz.
  • hihat closed: white noise highpassed at 7khz, 50ms decay.
  • hihat open: same source as closed but with a 250ms decay so it rings into the next step.
  • clap: four bursts of bandpassed noise spaced a few ms apart, with a slightly longer tail to fake a small room.

the sequencer schedules ahead with an interval timer and lookahead window, so timing does not drift even if the page is busy.
